Baked French Fries

Ingredients Needed:

  • 3 Medium sized potatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon of olive oil
  • 3/4 Teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 Teaspoon of pepper
  • 3/4 Teaspoon of garlic powder
  • Whatever other spices you so desire!

How to make:

  1. Start by washing and scrubbing your potatoes, then proceed to cut them into wedges.
  2. Get a bowl big enough to put all of your wedges in. After placing your potatoes in the bowl, start to cover them in water.
  3. Let your potatoes soak for 30 minutes, and while they soak, preheat your oven to 400f.
  4. When your done soaking your wedges, drain the water and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  5. After drying them coat the wedges in your oil and spices.
  6. Then grease a baking sheet and place the wedges on the sheet.
  7. Pop the sheet in the fully preheated oven for 45 minutes (Make sure to flip the wedges half way through).
  8. After 45 minutes, take out the fries and let them cool for 5 minutes and then serve!

Your fries are ready to be enjoyed! Eat them as is, or add cheese, or dip them in a sauce
