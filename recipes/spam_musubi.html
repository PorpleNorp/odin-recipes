Spam Musubi Recipe

Spam Musubi is a simple and filling recipe that needs about 4 simple things.

Ingredients needed:

  • Spam (as the name implies)
  • White rice
  • Nori sheet
  • Some form of sauce (I use teriyaki)
  • Not needed but suggested: Musubi press (You can also use your leftover spam can!)

How to make:

  1. Make some rice! I'm gonna assume you know how to already do this.
  2. Now what you want to do is slice your Spam into slices like the image above.
  3. Use medium heat on your skillet, and throw your slices on until they're nice and crispy.
  4. After you crisp both sides of the Spam, add your Teriyaki sauce.
  5. Let them get nice and coated and then turn off the heat.
  6. Next we line some plastic wrap into our Musubi press.
  7. Finally start to layer your rice into the press, after that add a slice of spam!
  8. Add one more layer of rice, and press the layered mixture together.
  9. Finally, cut your nori into strips long enough to wrap around your Musubi.
  10. After you wrap them, your Musubi is ready to eat.

With one just one can of spam you could probably make about 4 Musubi. That leaves you leftovers or some to share!

Hope you enjoy your sweet, savory, and salty snack!
